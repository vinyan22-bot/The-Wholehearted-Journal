Questions

Before you go

Is this a physical journal?

No — it's a digital PDF you download instantly after purchase. Print it at home, use a print-on-demand service, or write directly on it with a tablet and stylus app like GoodNotes or Notability.

Do I need to go through the modules in order?

No. Many people work front to back over about 90 days; others jump to whichever module matches the week they're having. Both are the intended way to use it.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. It's a self-reflection tool meant to support work you're already doing — on your own or alongside a therapist — not a clinical substitute. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a mental health professional or crisis line.

What size are the pages?

Standard US Letter (8.5" x 11"), formatted to print cleanly or fill in digitally at full resolution.
